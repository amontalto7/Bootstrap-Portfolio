About Me

Born and raised in Queens, NY, I've taken an interest in technology at an early age. In 5th grade, I took my first programming classes, learning Logo and Basic. I have about 17 years of experience in Quality Assurance, Automation testing, and coding. I'm currently working as a lead at SiriusXM on the Selenium Automation Team.

I'm currently taking a full stack web development boot camp at Columbia University in order to further my knowledge and skillset and get up to date on the latest technologies, best practices, and toolsets.

An avid traveler, comic book nerd, tech geek, foodie, and aspiring digital nomad, I'm excited for all the possibilities that will open up as this journey continues.
